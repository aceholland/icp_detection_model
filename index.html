// How It Works
🎥
rPPG Signal Extraction
MediaPipe FaceMesh detects 468 landmarks. The CHROM algorithm + EfficientPhys pretrained model extracts your pulse signal from subtle skin colour changes — invisible to the naked eye.
👁
Pupillometry + PLR Test
Iris landmarks track pupil diameter in real time. A 400ms screen flash triggers the pupillary light reflex — measuring constriction velocity and computing the NPI score clinically.
🧠
ICP Estimation
HR, SDNN, RMSSD, P2/P1 ratio, respiratory rate, and NPI feed into an XGBoost ensemble model trained on MIMIC-III waveform data. Output: ICP in mmHg with risk classification.
⚖
Bias-Aware Design
rPPG underperforms on darker skin tones (Fitzpatrick V–VI). NeuraScan uses CHROM chrominance cancellation and near-infrared-aware normalisation trained on diverse datasets.
📋
Clinical Report
Gemini Pro generates a concise clinical assessment from the extracted features and ICP estimate — framed for emergency triage use by paramedics or first-responders.
🌍
Zero Hardware
Runs in any browser with a webcam. No chest electrodes, no finger clips, no ultrasound probe. Designed for rural India, sub-Saharan Africa, and resource-limited emergency settings.
